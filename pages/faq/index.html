Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sell ships based on EGOTech blueprints?

No.

Can I share EGOTech blueprints?

For the free blueprints, you can share it with as many people as you want, just don't pass them off as your own.

For blueprints that are purchased, the purchase only allows for the unlimited use for one person. If you wish to have a company or group license, please let me know and we can work something out.

Do I get access to future updates for blueprint purchases?

Yes. A blueprint purchase provides access to all future updates to the ship's blueprints.

How can I provide feedback?

I can be found in-game as Egomaniac and on discord as @vinteo81 (vinteo#4211). Feel free to contact me and provide feedback or if you need help.

You can also join the community at the EGOTech Discord server.
